02 — ABOUT

Hello

I'm Marilyn—I help products speak human.

I've spent the last decade figuring out what things should say. Buttons, error messages, onboarding flows, entire content systems—the small words that shape how people feel when they use a product.

I've worked with startups finding their voice and enterprises untangling theirs. Lately, I've been curious about how AI is changing the way we write for (and with) machines.

When I'm not writing, I'm probably editing. Or deleting. Mostly deleting.
