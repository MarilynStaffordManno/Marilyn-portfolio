Scope
Financial UX, Accessibility

Making financial products feel human and approachable—because money is emotional, and the words around it should acknowledge that.

The Challenge

Financial services are often cold, confusing, and full of jargon. Discover wanted to stand apart by making their digital experience feel genuinely helpful—not like a bank talking at you, but like a knowledgeable friend helping you navigate your finances.

The Approach

We rewrote key flows with a focus on clarity and empathy. Credit limit increases, payment reminders, fraud alerts—each touchpoint was an opportunity to reduce anxiety and build trust through language that was direct, warm, and judgment-free.
